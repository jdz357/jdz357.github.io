This paper presents and evaluates several variational formulations for two-dimensional nonlinear elasticity using the discontinuous Petrov-Galerkin method. The formulations are solved with Newton–Raphson iteration and verified using manufactured solutions and the Cook’s Membrane benchmark problem.
