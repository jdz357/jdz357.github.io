This conference paper investigates how structural dynamics can induce electrical chatter in a pin-and-receptacle contact assembly. It combines experiments with high-fidelity structural simulations and linear and nonlinear reduced-order models to study the relationship between mechanical vibration, contact behavior, and interruptions in electrical continuity.
