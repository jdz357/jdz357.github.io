I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computational Science, Engineering, and Mathematics at the [Oden Institute](https://www.oden.utexas.edu/) at The University of Texas at Austin. I work with [Leszek Demkowicz](https://users.oden.utexas.edu/~leszek/) and [George Biros](https://oden.utexas.edu/people/directory/george-biros/), and my research focuses on finite element methods, numerical methods for partial differential equations, high-performance computing, and nonlinear problems.

My current work studies variational formulations for nonlinear elasticity, particularly the discontinuous Petrov–Galerkin method. I am also interested in connecting stable numerical formulations with machine learning. Beyond academia, I have worked in quantitative research and model risk at JPMorganChase and previously studied structural dynamics and reduced-order modeling at Sandia National Laboratories.
